So how does it work?

In addition to printing to a local printer connected to your computer, Google Chrome can also print to Google Cloud Print. When you print to Cloud Print, Chrome sends a PDF of your document to Google's servers. Then, the PDF is downloaded by a custom python script and sent to Northeastern's print queue. You will receive an email in 2-10 minutes when the document is ready to print. Documents are added to the same Northeastern print queue when printed with the Pharos driver from MyNEU or sent to mobileprinting@neu.edu. Once they are in this queue, you can go to any printer on Northeastern's campus, swipe your husky card, and print away!
